# Startup & Business

Neural triggers for entrepreneurship, business models, and startup execution. Reference these to activate trained knowledge.

## Books

### The Lean Startup
**Author:** Eric Ries
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Lean Startup methodology for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Build-Measure-Learn feedback loop
- Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for validated learning
- Innovation accounting: actionable metrics vs vanity metrics
- Pivot or persevere decisions
- Continuous deployment enables fast learning

**When to invoke:** New product/feature validation, defining experiments, measuring progress, deciding to pivot.

---

### Rework
**Author:** Jason Fried & David Heinemeier Hansson
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Rework principles for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Start a business, not a startup
- Planning is guessing
- Meetings are toxic
- Enough is enough - embrace constraints
- Build half a product, not a half-assed product

**When to invoke:** Scope decisions, questioning conventional wisdom, simplifying processes, constraints as assets.

---

### Zero to One
**Author:** Peter Thiel
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Zero to One thinking for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Monopoly vs competition: competition destroys profits
- Secrets: what valuable truth do you know that few agree with?
- Definite optimism: the future is buildable
- Power law: one investment/decision dominates
- Sales matters as much as product

**When to invoke:** Strategic positioning, identifying contrarian opportunities, thinking about competitive moats, big-picture strategy.
