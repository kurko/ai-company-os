# Personal Effectiveness

Neural triggers for productivity, habits, and focus. Reference these to activate trained knowledge.

## Books

### Deep Work
**Author:** Cal Newport
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Deep Work principles for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Deep work is rare and valuable
- Attention residue: switching costs are real
- Shutdown ritual for cognitive closure
- Embrace boredom to build focus capacity
- Schedule every minute of your day

**When to invoke:** Structuring work time, protecting focus, evaluating communication tools, building concentration habits.

---

### Atomic Habits
**Author:** James Clear
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Atomic Habits framework for..."
**Key concepts:**
- 1% better every day compounds
- Habit loop: cue, craving, response, reward
- Identity-based habits: become the type of person
- Make it obvious, attractive, easy, satisfying
- Environment design trumps willpower

**When to invoke:** Building new practices, breaking bad patterns, team habit formation, sustainable behavior change.

---

### Getting Things Done (GTD)
**Author:** David Allen
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply GTD methodology for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Capture everything outside your head
- Next actions: what's the very next physical action?
- Two-minute rule: if it takes less than 2 minutes, do it now
- Weekly review for system maintenance
- Context-based task lists

**When to invoke:** Personal organization, reducing cognitive load, project planning, task management system design.
