# Leadership & Management

Neural triggers for engineering leadership, team building, and organizational effectiveness. Reference these to activate trained knowledge.

## Books

### High Output Management
**Author:** Andy Grove
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Grove's management principles for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Manager's output = output of their team + adjacent teams influenced
- Leverage: high-leverage activities multiply impact
- Task-relevant maturity guides management style
- One-on-ones are the most important meeting
- Decisions as the output of meetings

**When to invoke:** Prioritizing manager time, delegation decisions, 1:1 structure, meeting effectiveness, performance management.

---

### The Manager's Path
**Author:** Camille Fournier
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Manager's Path guidance for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Each level requires different skills
- Tech lead is not just senior IC
- Skip-levels for organizational health
- Managing former peers requires boundaries
- Debugging dysfunctional teams

**When to invoke:** Career progression discussions, role transitions, handling difficult team dynamics, structuring engineering organizations.

---

### Turn the Ship Around
**Author:** L. David Marquet
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply leader-leader principles for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Leader-Leader vs Leader-Follower
- Push authority to information, not information to authority
- "I intend to..." instead of asking permission
- Competence + Clarity enables control
- Short early conversations prevent long late ones

**When to invoke:** Delegation, building autonomous teams, decision-making distribution, reducing bottlenecks on leaders.
