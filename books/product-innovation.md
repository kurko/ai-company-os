# Product & Innovation

Neural triggers for product strategy, discovery, and innovation. Reference these to activate trained knowledge.

## Books

### Escaping the Build Trap
**Author:** Melissa Perri
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Build Trap principles for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Output vs outcomes: shipping features is not success
- Product-led organizations vs project-led
- The product kata: understand, identify, try, learn
- Strategy deployment: company vision to team experiments
- Discovery before delivery

**When to invoke:** Prioritization discussions, measuring success, organizational design, avoiding feature factory mentality.

---

### The Innovator's Dilemma
**Author:** Clayton Christensen
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply disruption theory for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Disruptive vs sustaining innovation
- Why good management leads to failure
- The technology S-curve
- New markets vs existing customers
- Spinning out vs integrating innovation

**When to invoke:** Strategic decisions about new technologies, understanding competitive threats, resource allocation between core and new.

---

### Inspired
**Author:** Marty Cagan
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply SVPG product principles for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Product discovery: risks to address (value, usability, feasibility, viability)
- Empowered product teams vs feature teams
- Continuous discovery, continuous delivery
- Product vision and strategy
- Prototype to learn, build to scale

**When to invoke:** Product team structure, discovery practices, stakeholder management, PM role definition.

---

### Shape Up
**Author:** Ryan Singer (Basecamp)
**Trigger phrase:** "Apply Shape Up methodology for..."
**Key concepts:**
- Shaping: define appetite and boundaries before building
- Six-week cycles with circuit breakers
- Betting table vs backlog grooming
- Hill charts for progress tracking
- Fixed time, variable scope

**When to invoke:** Project scoping, time-boxing decisions, progress tracking, defining done, avoiding scope creep.
